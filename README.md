### 🦖 About Me

I'm a **Full Stack Developer** with nearly **3 years of professional experience** engineering robust, scalable, and maintainable applications. I thrive on solving complex problems and taking projects from a blank canvas to a fully deployed modular solution.

- 🏗️ **Core Expertise:** Deep knowledge of **software architecture, design patterns, and clean code**. I specialize in structuring modular projects from scratch and optimizing performance through **parallel programming** and efficient **algorithms**.
- 💻 **Main Stack:** Highly proficient in the `.NET` ecosystem (C#, ASP.NET) alongside modern JavaScript/TypeScript frameworks like `React` and `Next.js`, plus `Node.js` and `Python`.
- 📊 **Hobby & Complementary Skills:** As a strong complement to my developer role, I'm passionate about **Data Science** and **Business Intelligence**. I leverage tools like **Power BI** and **Power Automate**, and I'm actively expanding my cloud infrastructure expertise with **Azure** (studying for AZ-900) and **Oracle Cloud**.
